Nutrition Plans, what you need to know

The nutrition industry is the biggest industry in the world for misinformation and scaremongering. It seems every magazine, Tv channel or social media outlet have conflicting information on what’s the best diet to lose weight or what’s a healthy food What’s a bad food etc. The problem with nutrition is it is so confusing for the public to know who to listen too. You have people doing 12-week courses and claiming to be “Nutritionists” who sell “Diet plans” when in truth they haven’t a clue what their talking about, they simply regurgitate information they have learned (often misinformation) and don’t take into account specific differences and needs between clients. The truth is nutrition is a science so there shouldn’t be a difference in opinion, there should only be evidence-based protocols. At Crookes Nutrition and Fitness, you have the peace of mind knowing you’re getting legitimate evidence-based advice that works.

At Crookes Nutrition & Fitness we specialise in evidence-based nutrition (DIP, ISSN, CISSN). You will never fall victim to a money-making diet scam again with the education you will receive with any of our nutrition packages. None of our plans have any absolute rules; No syns, No demonising of food groups, No detoxes, No setting you up for failure, No yo-yo diet culture, No judgement, No bullshit, No money grabbing scams just results and knowledge to last a lifetime.
