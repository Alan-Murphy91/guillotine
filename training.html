PERSONAL TRAINING
      

Why should you invest in personal training with us?

Personal trainers are everywhere, but qualified and conscientious ones are rare. So many gyms and trainers present you with a generic, copy and paste, programme which takes no consideration of your goals, preferences, current fitness levels, injuries or movement patterns. You go through the motions, they take your cash and you end up with little or no results. Sound familiar? The mission of Crookes Nutrition & Fitness is to ensure you get the exact opposite of the generic personal training. The gym has been designed to facilitate expert personal training. It is kitted out with world class equipment but, most importantly, our goal is to ensure your personal training truly is personal.

Your plan will be made exclusively for you, dictated by your goals. In Crookes Nutrition & Fitness it’s just you and your trainer, both working towards the goal of getting you results. We offer a relaxed, friendly atmosphere where you will get the results you want. You’ll learn correct form; how to perform exercises perfectly. You will learn how to optimally stimulate every muscle fibre. You’ll get a personal training experience the way it should be: a trainer who genuinely cares and wants to see you succeed.
